Emulators on iOS

Classic gaming consoles in your pocket.


RetroArch

All-in-one emulator supporting over 30 systems with advanced features like shaders, netplay, rewinding, and more.


Provenance

iOS & tvOS multi-emulator frontend, supporting Atari, Bandai, NEC, Nintendo, Sega, SNK and Sony console systems.


Delta

Classic video game emulator for iOS devices, including support for the NES, SNES, N64, GBC, GBA, and DS.


Mame4iOS

Multi Arcade Machine Emulator supporting over 2,000 games on iOS, TvOS, and MacOS.


PPSSPP

Open-Source PSP emulator supporting many platforms, inluding iOS.


DolphiniOS

The popular Dolphin Emulator ported to iOS devices. Supports GameCube and Wii systems.


uoYabause

Unofficial port of Yabause SEGA Saturn Emulator for Android and iOS.


Play!

PS2 Emulator for iOS.


FlyCast

Native DreamCast emulator for iOS.


Happy Chick Emulator

All-in-one emulator supporting over 18 different video game systems.
